All World ETF Portfolio

Number of funds held: 6-11

Relative Volatility: 0.9 to 1.3

The All World ETF Portfolio seeks long-term growth by investing in Index ETF Funds provided by iShares and Fidelity. Usually it will hold between 6 to 11 different ETFs at one time. A majority of the holdings will be domestic equities, but international and bond ETFs will also be held depending on market conditions. International holding can make up to 40% of the portfolio at any given time. Additionally, in an effort to reduce portfolio risk, up to 20% of the portfolio could be held in fixed income ETFs. Diversified domestic equity and international funds will be held and for domestic equities industry based ETFs will also be held to target oppurtunities that are seen in certain industry groups. The use of ETFs keeps management fees minimized, but on occasion actively managed ETFs with higher fees will be held if they present a better oppurtunity to passively indexed ETFs. Common valuation metrics, which include Price/Earnings, Price/Sales, and Earnings Growth will be used to determine investment oppurtunities. Typically, the All World ETF Portfolio holds a large basket of stock positions, and will remain fully invested a majority of the time. It competes with the S&P 500 index. During bearish market conditions the portfolio may become more defensive, but money market funds are not usually held.
