We aim to keep advisory fees low by having an accelerated decline in our fee for every $100,000 up to our lowest breakpoint of 0.4% for any dollar above $400,000. Our fee is calculated based on the size of your entire portfolio under our management. In addition, we only use Fidelity mutual funds that offer some of the lowest expense ratios and are no-load funds. We strive to minimize fees, so that your returns are maximized.
